AlphaLab · consolidated UX mockups · design revision v1.9.21

One bench, two rosters, every number accountable.

The single current mockup set. It replaces the earlier per-topic files. Two rosters share one arena, the mathematical seat and the AI seat, judged by the same rails, with a deferred advisor seat shown for completeness. The signature element is the drawn MDE interval gauge: no judged number appears without the bracket saying what the data can and cannot see (UX-1, rule 22). All honesty logic stays in the D58 read-models; every screen renders fields verbatim. It covers both halves of the lab: the operational book (screens 04-07, the live paper trading, portfolio, positions, orders and fills, cash and equity) and the judging machinery (leaderboard, verdicts, attribution, the AI seats, audit, allocation, risk, data health, research, replay). Layout is fluid and single-column, so a future mobile client is a styling pass, not a rebuild, and screen 18 shows the mobile frame the design already anticipates.
